 # customer-journey.md

## Aware

### Trigger Event
- Discovery of the need for a standardized AI usage verification tool in the market.
- Increased scrutiny and demand for transparency in AI usage in products and services.

### Friction Points
- Lack of awareness about the existence of such a tool.
- Difficulty in understanding the benefits and importance of AI usage verification.

### User Emotions
- Curiosity and interest.
- Skepticism and doubt.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and concise explanation of the problem and the solution provided by the AI-Usage-Verifier.
- Demonstration of the tool's effectiveness and ease of use.

### Metric
- Number of unique visitors to the product website or landing page.

## Consider

### Trigger Event
- Further research and evaluation of the AI-Usage-Verifier.
- Comparison with other AI usage verification solutions in the market.

### Friction Points
- Complexity of the tool and its features.
- Lack of trust in the tool due to newness in the market.

### User Emotions
- Interest and excitement.
- Caution and hesitation.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Detailed documentation and tutorials on how to use the tool.
- Testimonials and case studies from satisfied customers.
- Free trial or demo of the tool.

### Metric
- Number of trial sign-ups or demos requested.

## Try

### Trigger Event
- Use of the AI-Usage-Verifier in a limited capacity or for a trial period.

### Friction Points
- Learning curve associated with using the tool.
- Technical issues or bugs during the trial period.

### User Emotions
- Frustration and annoyance.
- Satisfaction and relief.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Responsive and helpful customer support.
- Easy integration with existing systems and workflows.
- User-friendly interface and intuitive design.

### Metric
- Number of active trial users.
- User engagement and usage metrics.

## Adopt

### Trigger Event
- Decision to purchase the AI-Usage-Verifier for regular use.

### Friction Points
- Cost of the tool.
- Concerns about data security and privacy.

### User Emotions
- Confidence and trust.
- Excitement and anticipation.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Competitive pricing and flexible pricing plans.
- Strong data security and privacy measures.
- Ongoing support and updates.

### Metric
- Number of paying customers.
- Revenue generated.

## Expand

### Trigger Event
- Increased usage and adoption of the AI-Usage-Verifier.
- Requests for additional features or integrations.

### Friction Points
- Limited scalability of the tool.
- Difficulty in meeting customization requests.

### User Emotions
- Satisfaction and loyalty.
- Expectations for continued improvement and innovation.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Regular updates and improvements based on user feedback.
- Customization options to meet specific needs.
- Expansion of the tool's capabilities and integrations.

### Metric
- Increase in usage and engagement metrics.
- Customer satisfaction and loyalty metrics.
