## Positioning
**Primary Positioning:** "The gold standard for verifying and authenticating AI usage in products and services."

**Alternative Positionings:**
1. "Ensure trust and transparency in AI-powered products with our verification tool."
2. "The definitive solution for developers and marketers to prove genuine AI usage."
3. "Build trust in your AI products with our standardized verification and authentication tool."

1. **Persona:** Alex, the AI Product Manager

2. **Persona:** Sarah, the Developer Advocate

   - **Daily Job:** Mark develops marketing strategies for AI-powered products, ensuring they resonate with consumers and meet transparency standards.
