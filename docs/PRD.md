# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for ai-usage-verifier

## Problem Statement
The increasing adoption of AI in software products and services has raised concerns about transparency and trust. Developers, marketers, and consumers need a standardized way to verify and authenticate AI usage to ensure compliance, build trust, and provide accurate information to users.

## Target Users
- **Developers**: Need to ensure their AI components are properly verified and authenticated.
- **Marketers**: Need to communicate AI usage transparently to consumers.
- **Consumers**: Need to understand and trust the AI components in the products they use.

## Goals
- Provide a standardized way to verify and authenticate AI usage.
- Ensure transparency and trust in AI-powered products.
- Enable developers to easily integrate verification mechanisms.
- Help marketers communicate AI usage effectively.
- Build consumer trust by providing clear information about AI components.

## Key Features (Prioritized)

### High Priority
1. **AI Usage Verification**:
   - Develop a system to verify the usage of AI components in products.
   - Ensure verification is standardized and reliable.

2. **Authentication Mechanism**:
   - Implement a secure authentication system to confirm the authenticity of AI components.
   - Provide developers with tools to authenticate their AI components.

3. **Developer Integration**:
   - Create SDKs and APIs for easy integration with existing products.
   - Provide documentation and examples for developers.

4. **Marketer Communication**:
   - Develop templates and guidelines for marketers to communicate AI usage.
   - Provide tools to generate transparent and clear communication materials.

5. **Consumer Information**:
   - Create a user-friendly interface to display AI usage information.
   - Ensure information is clear, concise, and easy to understand.

### Medium Priority
1. **Compliance Tools**:
   - Develop tools to help developers ensure compliance with AI regulations.
   - Provide resources and guidelines for compliance.

2. **Audit Logs**:
   - Implement audit logs to track AI usage verification and authentication.
   - Provide developers with access to audit logs for transparency.

### Low Priority
1. **Advanced Analytics**:
   - Develop advanced analytics to provide insights into AI usage patterns.
   - Offer reports and dashboards for developers and marketers.

2. **Community Support**:
   - Build a community forum for developers to share best practices and solutions.
   - Provide support and resources for marketers and consumers.

## Success Metrics
- **Developer Adoption**: Number of developers integrating the verification system.
- **Marketer Engagement**: Number of marketers using the communication tools.
- **Consumer Trust**: Feedback and satisfaction from consumers regarding transparency.
- **Compliance Rate**: Percentage of developers ensuring compliance with AI regulations.
- **Audit Log Usage**: Number of developers using audit logs for transparency.

## Scope
- **In Scope**:
  - AI usage verification and authentication.
  - Developer integration tools (SDKs, APIs, documentation).
  - Marketer communication tools and templates.
  - Consumer information interface.
  - Compliance tools and resources.
  - Audit logs for tracking AI usage.

- **Out of Scope**:
  - Development of AI components themselves.
  - Advanced analytics and community support in the initial release.
  - Integration with third-party AI platforms beyond the initial target ecosystem.

## Assumptions
- Developers will find the integration tools user-friendly and comprehensive.
- Marketers will find the communication tools helpful and effective.
- Consumers will appreciate the transparency provided by the information interface.
- Compliance tools will be useful and effective for developers.
- Audit logs will be valuable for developers to track AI usage.

## Risks
- **Integration Complexity**: Ensuring the verification system is easy to integrate with existing products.
- **Consumer Trust**: Building and maintaining consumer trust in the verification process.
- **Compliance Challenges**: Keeping up with evolving AI regulations and ensuring compliance.
- **Audit Log Security**: Ensuring the security and integrity of audit logs.

## Dependencies
- **Developer Tools**: Availability and reliability of SDKs and APIs for integration.
- **Marketer Resources**: Availability of templates and guidelines for marketers.
- **Consumer Interface**: Development of a user-friendly interface for consumers.
- **Compliance Tools**: Availability of resources and guidelines for compliance.
- **Audit Logs**: Implementation and maintenance of secure audit logs.

## Timeline
- **Phase 1 (0-3 months)**: Planning and design.
- **Phase 2 (3-6 months)**: Development of core features (verification, authentication, integration tools).
- **Phase 3 (6-9 months)**: Development of marketer and consumer tools.
- **Phase 4 (9-12 months)**: Testing, compliance, and launch.

## Budget
- **Development**: $200,000
- **Marketing**: $50,000
- **Support**: $30,000
- **Total**: $280,000

## Conclusion
The ai-usage-verifier tool aims to provide a standardized way to verify and authenticate AI usage in products or services, ensuring transparency and trust among developers, marketers, and consumers. By prioritizing key features and setting clear success metrics, we aim to deliver a valuable and user-friendly solution that meets the needs of our target users.
